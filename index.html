About the company

Family-run since 1993, still on the shop floor.

Prabhakar Processors is based in Kadodara, on Surat’s textile belt, one of India’s busiest fabric-processing corridors. We work as a family, not a hierarchy, which is why timelines get kept and shades get matched, order after order.

The plant runs the latest dyeing and finishing machinery available for the job, so a single fabric length can be sheared, sueded, and treated for water repellency, soil release or stain release, in addition to being dyed or printed to spec.

  • FIBRES Manmade, blended & natural fabrics
  • FINISH Shearing, sueding, water-repellent & stain-release treatments
  • TEAM 100+ skilled dyeing, printing & finishing hands
  • SCALE 700+ clients served since 1993
Our journey

Three decades on the same stretch of Bardoli Road.

Prabhakar Processors has grown one process, one machine and one client relationship at a time since 1993 — still run by the same family that started it.

1993

The unit is founded

A family-run dyeing unit opens in Kadodara on Surat’s Bardoli Road, starting with piece and solid dyeing for the local weaving trade.

Growth years

Cationic dyeing & capacity added

As manmade fibre volumes rose across Surat, cationic dyeing capacity was added and the solid-dyeing lines were scaled up to keep pace with year-round demand.

Printing added

Screen & discharge printing brought in-house

Printing capability was built alongside the dyeing floor, so patterned orders no longer had to travel to a separate unit.

Finishing & digital

In-house finishing and digital printing

Shearing, sueding and water-repellent, soil-release & stain-release finishing moved in-house, and digital printing was added for short runs and sampling.

Today

700+ clients, six processes, one roof

The same family still runs day-to-day operations, backed by a team of 100+ skilled dyeing, printing and finishing hands.

What we process

A-to-Z dyeing and printing, under one roof.

Whatever the fibre, the order or the finish, it’s handled here, not subcontracted out. That's what keeps 700+ clients coming back to Kadodara.

01

Piece Dyeing

Fabric-length dyeing for manmade, blended and natural cloth, matched to shade with consistency across every metre of the lot.

02

Solid Dyeing

Even, saturated single-tone colour for orders that need one uncompromising shade, lot after lot.

03

Cationic Dyeing

Deep, vivid colour on cationic-dyeable polyester: the shade range that plain disperse dyeing can’t reach.

04

Screen Printing

Flat and rotary screen work for repeat patterns, run at production scale without losing definition.

05

Discharge Printing

Pattern printed by removing colour from a dyed ground, for a crisp, light-on-dark look screen printing alone can’t give.

06

Digital Printing

Photographic detail and short-run flexibility for designs too intricate, or too small a lot, for screens.

How we do it

From grey fabric to finished roll.

Stage 1

Fabric intake & shade matching

Grey or ready fabric arrives and is checked against the shade card or swatch the client has approved before a single litre of dye is mixed.

Stage 2

Dyeing or printing

Piece, solid or cationic dyeing for solid-shade orders; screen, discharge or digital printing for patterned work, run on the machinery suited to that fibre and design.

Stage 3

Finishing

Shearing or sueding for hand-feel, plus water-repellent, soil-release or stain-release treatment where the end use calls for it.

Stage 4

Quality check & dispatch

Every lot is checked for shade consistency and finish before it leaves Kadodara for the client.
